Welcome to the Keyblades page!

In the Kingdom Hearts series, Keyblades are magical weapons that can unlock any lock and are wielded by the main characters. Each Keyblade has unique abilities and designs, often reflecting the character's journey or the world they are associated with.

List of Keyblades

Here is a list of notable Keyblades from the Kingdom Hearts series:

  • Kingdom Key
    • Details

    The iconic Keyblade that Sora wields, representing the light and the beginning of his journey.

  • Oathkeeper
    • Details

    A Keyblade that symbolizes Sora's promise to Kairi, known for its strength and light-based abilities.

  • Oblivion
    • Details

    A dark counterpart to Oathkeeper, representing Sora's connection to Riku and the darkness within him.

  • Ultima Weapon
    • Details

    The most powerful Keyblade in the series, often requiring significant effort to obtain, representing the pinnacle of Sora's strength.

  • Kingdom Key D
    • Details

    A Keyblade associated with Riku, representing his journey and connection to darkness.
